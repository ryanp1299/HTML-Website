Bull Case for Palantir Stock

Palantir is a software as a service company looking to change the world by offering data software for the modern enterprise. They have provided software/data services for the US government for several decades. Palantir is looking to bring the expertise that they have developed to the commercial market. Foundry is their product that will allow private sector companies the ability to organize their operational data and use machine learning to help them make important decisions. With a projected 30% year over year growth rate for the next several years, I believe this company is a buy.
